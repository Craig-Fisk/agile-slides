In an Agile world how do you lead,
motivate and inspire your teams?

I like to keep my communication simple and concise

(hence this very short simple presentation)
.

3 Principles

I use 3 key principles to lead, motivate and inspire my teams

Communication

Possibly the most important one of all.

  • Effective agile teams require excellent communication
    • Inter team (Agile ceremonies, day to day)
    • Team <----> Team (Support, delivery)
    • Team <----> leadership (Goals, stategy)

Simple. Clear. Concise.

Mentorship

When people are managers too many think it means people are working for them and when they're a mentor they think they're working for the other person.
I believe all the best managers are actually mentors.

- Gary Vaynerchuk (DailyVee 208)

Empathy

Entering every conversation with empathy leads to better communication

  • Short term
    • What does my team need out of this conversation?
    • Why does my team care about this decision?
    • What is frustrating my team?
  • Long term
    • What makes my team passionate?
    • What are my team's individual goals?
    • What is going to keep my team happy?
